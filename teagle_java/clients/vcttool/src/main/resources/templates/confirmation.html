Depending on the expected duration of the booking, you may choose to request that the process be started asynchronously by ticking the option below. In this case, the request processor will complete the booking in the background, immediately returning control. Regardless of the chosen method, the state of the booking is afterwards accessible from the toolbar buttons.

After the booking completes, a summary will be available, with the details for accessing the provisioned components. This information is just a printable version of the configuration data for all included components. The log produced by the orchestration engine will also be made available.
